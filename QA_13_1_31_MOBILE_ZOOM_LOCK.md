# QA Checklist: 13.1.31 Mobile Zoom Lock

## Mobile Browser

- Open the app on an iPhone or Android phone.
- Try pinch-zooming on the login screen. The page should not zoom.
- Log in and try pinch-zooming on Today, Staff Roster, Inventory, and Time Clock screens. The page should not zoom.
- Double-tap buttons/cards quickly. The app should not zoom in.
- Tap text inputs, textareas, and dropdowns. The phone should not zoom the field.
- Confirm normal vertical scrolling still works.

## Desktop

- Open the app on desktop.
- Confirm normal layout and scrolling are unchanged.
- Confirm mouse/trackpad use still works normally.

## Regression Check

- Confirm login still works.
- Confirm Staff Roster still opens.
- Confirm Time Clock buttons still respond.
- Confirm System Administrator still opens for authorized users.
